Our Story
Knowledge matters only when it is put into action.

Praxis Advisory Consultants was founded on a simple conviction: that the most persistent failures in development are not failures of knowledge, but failures of translation — the inability to move rigorous evidence from the report to the policy, from the policy to the programme, and from the programme to the person it was meant to serve.

The firm brings together deep experience in governance and public policy, monitoring and evaluation, climate governance, and sustainability. Our founders have worked inside the multilateral and bilateral systems — at GIZ, UNDP, UNEP, UNFPA, and government — and have seen both what works and what gets lost in translation.

Praxis Advisory Consultants was built to do both: to think rigorously and to act practically. We combine analytical depth with implementation accountability — designing and evaluating programmes, drafting policy, building results frameworks, and executing projects on the ground. Senior specialists lead every assignment, and we bring in vetted sectoral experts where the assignment demands it.

Our work spans the full policy-to-delivery chain. We believe the most durable development results come from not just knowing what works, but ensuring it gets done — with the evidence, the systems, and the accountability in place to prove it.

Our Services

Six practice areas,
one accountable team.

From policy analysis to field implementation, we work across the full development value chain — always with senior specialists in the lead.

01
Practice Area

Policy, Governance & Institutional Reform

Policy Research & Formulation Governance Advisory Institutional Reform Public Sector Management Regulatory Reform Civic Engagement

We provide analytical, advisory and technical assistance support across the policy cycle — from evidence synthesis and policy formulation, through to legal drafting, regulatory reform and institutional strengthening. Our governance work spans public sector accountability, parliamentary and judicial reform, decentralisation, and civic engagement.

Our team has led complex governance assignments for bilateral and multilateral clients across Pakistan, designing and supporting reform processes from the federal to the local level. We understand the political economy of reform — what works in theory, what gets stuck in practice, and how to navigate the gap.

We work as technical advisors embedded within government reform processes, and as independent evaluators assessing institutional performance. Our approach is always grounded in local context — drawing on primary research, stakeholder analysis, and deep familiarity with Pakistan's public sector landscape.

02
Practice Area

Monitoring, Evaluation, Research & Learning

M&E Framework Design Log Frame Development Surveys & Data Collection Quantitative Analysis Qualitative Analysis Results-Based Monitoring Donor Reporting

Our MERL practice covers the full results chain: theory of change and log frame design, indicator selection, baseline and endline surveys, mid-term and final evaluations, and learning systems. We design and manage mixed-methods data collection, and produce rigorous, donor-grade analytical products — from rapid assessments to multi-year programme evaluations.

We have designed and delivered MERL systems for programmes funded by GIZ, UNDP and other major donors. We understand both the technical requirements of results-based management and the practical realities of data collection in complex, low-resource settings.

Our analytical capabilities span survey design and sampling, statistical analysis, and in-depth qualitative work including key informant interviews, focus groups, participatory assessments, and case studies. Every product is written to donor-grade standards and structured around decisions rather than data.

03
Practice Area

Climate, Environment & Sustainability

Climate Budgeting & Governance Climate Adaptation Plans Climate Risk & Vulnerability ESG Advisory Circular Economy Textile Circularity Solid Waste Management Life-cycle Assessment Green Economy & Green Jobs

We combine climate science with policy acuity. Our work covers climate budgeting and public financial management, National Adaptation Plans (NAPs), climate risk and vulnerability assessments, ESG advisory, circular economy and textile circularity, solid waste management, life-cycle assessment, and green economy and green jobs programming.

Pakistan is among the countries most vulnerable to climate change, and our climate practice is built specifically around the challenges of the South Asian context. We have supported government counterparts, UN agencies and bilateral donors on climate mainstreaming, adaptation planning, and green economy transitions.

Our circular economy work spans the full value chain — from policy frameworks and regulatory design through to industry engagement, producer responsibility schemes, textile circularity, and waste management system strengthening. We are among the few Pakistani firms with dedicated expertise in this area.

04
Practice Area

Youth, Skills & Human Development

Youth Employment TVET Reform Skills Development Human Development Labour Market Analysis Social Protection

From TVET system reform to youth employment programmes and social protection analysis, we support the full human development spectrum. We work at the intersection of policy and delivery — designing skills programmes, evaluating labour market interventions, and building frameworks that connect young people to productive opportunities.

Our TVET work has been shaped by direct engagement with Pakistan's national skills system, including work on NAVTTC frameworks and provincial-level TVET reform. We understand the political economy of skills governance — the gaps between policy intent and delivery reality that determine whether skills programmes actually reach young people.

We approach youth employment as a systemic challenge, not a programme problem. We analyse labour markets, map skill demands, and support the design of interventions that are grounded in employer needs and realistic about the barriers young people face in entering and sustaining productive work.

05
Practice Area

Programme Design & Implementation

Project Design Programme Implementation Stakeholder Analysis Field Coordination Results-Based Management Adaptive Management

We support clients from concept to close-out: participatory programme design, project planning and management, stakeholder engagement, field coordination, and results-based monitoring. We serve as prime implementers, technical advisors or sub-contractors depending on the assignment structure and client needs.

Programme design at Praxis Advisory Consultants begins with a clear theory of change and a realistic implementation model — one that accounts for context, the capacities of implementing partners, and the political economy of the sector. We do not produce designs that work only on paper.

Our project management practice combines formal methodology with the adaptive management approaches required in complex development settings. We manage multi-stakeholder, multi-location projects and build the monitoring and learning systems needed to detect and respond to implementation challenges in real time.

06
Practice Area

Knowledge, Communications & Engagement

Policy Research Advocacy Strategic Communication Stakeholder Engagement Donor Reporting Knowledge Products

Research and strategy create value only when they reach the people who need to act on them. We produce policy briefs, knowledge products and advocacy materials, and support clients with stakeholder analysis and engagement, strategic communications, and donor and project reporting.

We translate complex analytical findings into clear, actionable policy products — writing for policymakers, programme managers and donor audiences. Our knowledge products are designed to communicate, not just report: structured around decisions, not data.

Our stakeholder engagement work spans mapping and analysis, consultation design, advocacy strategy, and coalition building. We help clients understand who needs to be at the table, what they need to hear, and how to sustain their engagement over the life of a programme or reform process.

Our Delivery Model

The associate model.

For assignments requiring specialist depth beyond our principal expertise — in health, WASH, energy, social protection, statistics and other sectors — we work with a vetted network of associate experts engaged on an assignment-by-assignment basis. This gives clients access to the right expertise for each task, without the overhead of a large permanent team. All associates operate under our ethics, safeguarding, data protection and quality assurance standards.
